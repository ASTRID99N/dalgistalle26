The Philosophy

Three things we
will never compromise on.

01
◈

Sterling Silver.
Nothing Else.

Every single piece in every single drop is sterling silver. Not silver-plated. Not silver-toned. Not "silver-inspired." Sterling. 925. The real thing. We chose silver because it ages with you — it gets more interesting the longer you wear it, just like the girl who wears it.

925 sterling silver · always
02
♛

26 Pieces.
Every Drop.

Every collection is limited to exactly 26 pieces. The number is not random — it's the founding number of this brand, woven into its name and its DNA. 26 pieces means the girl who gets one is not everyone. She is someone who decided she wanted it before it was gone.

26 pieces · per drop · always
03
✦

No Restock.
No Exceptions.

When the 26 pieces are gone, they're gone. We don't restock. We don't make exceptions for anyone. Not for DMs. Not for emails. Not for "I was going to buy it but I waited." The girl who has it, has it. The girl who didn't — she'll be ready for the next drop.

sold out means sold out · forever

Why Silver

Silver doesn't
pretend to be
something else.

Gold asks for your attention. Silver just exists — cold, clean, impossibly elegant. It doesn't shout. It doesn't need to. It sits on your wrist or around your neck and lets you do the talking. That's why every DalgiStälle26 piece is silver. It matches the energy of the girl who wears it.

925
Sterling silver purity · The only standard we accept

"Silver is the metal of girls who don't need to announce themselves. It announces them for free."

— DalgiStälle26

The Drop Rules

How every drop works.
No exceptions. Ever.

◈

26 pieces announced

Every drop starts with an announcement — date, time, and a preview of the pieces. That's your warning. Use it.

◇

Drop goes live

At the exact time posted. No early access. No VIP queue. Everyone starts at the same moment — it's just about who moves first.

◉

26 sold = drop closed

The moment the 26th piece sells, the drop is over. The page goes dark. No waitlist. No "notify me." It's done.

✦

Next drop. New pieces.

Sold-out pieces never return. The next drop brings entirely new designs. Dalgi doesn't repeat herself — and neither do we.
